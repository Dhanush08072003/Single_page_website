Smart Projects

Precision Irrigation System

Objective: Enhance water efficiency and optimize crop yield through precise irrigation.
Features: IoT-enabled soil moisture sensors, automated irrigation scheduling, and real-time monitoring.
Results: Reduced water consumption by 20%, increased crop yield by 15%.

Smart Greenhouse Automation:

Objective: Optimize greenhouse conditions for maximum crop growth.
Features: Automated climate control, sensors for monitoring humidity and temperature, remote control via mobile app.
Results: Increased yield, improved energy efficiency

Blockchain-Based Supply Chain Tracking:

Objective: Ensure transparency and traceability in the agricultural supply chain.
Features: Blockchain technology for recording and verifying transactions, QR code-based tracking for produce.
Results: Enhanced trust among consumers, improved traceability for quality control.
